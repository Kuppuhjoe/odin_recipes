Taiwanese Dumplings

DESCRIPTION

A dumpling recipe direct from a Taiwanese friend.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 heads napa cabbage finely chopped
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 2 pounds ground pork
  • 3 jalapeno peppers minces
  • 2 large egg whites
>

STEPS

  1. Spread cabbage and cilantro over clean dish towel
  2. Transfer cabbage and cilantro to a large bowl and mix ground pork, jalapeno peppers, etc.
  3. Line 2 baking sheets with waxed paper
  4. Hold potsticker wrapper in palm of your hand and wrap a spoonful of ingredients in it
  5. Place dumplings in freezer until firm and cook
